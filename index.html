I work on the type of marketing that helps brands connect with people and drive results — building strategies that resonate with current marketing trends.

My experience spans co-branded product launches, audience growth, creator-to-consumer conversion pipelines, and multi-channel campaigns. Every brief I take on starts with one question: what will make this audience act?

Currently at IIT Roorkee, I work with brands and student organisations to plan and execute campaigns — exploring competitor strategies and market trends before a single creative goes live.

I treat every brief as a research problem first — because strategy without insight is just decoration.
